Cheesy Zucchini Casserole

Description

Cheesy Zucchini Casserole is a comforting, veggie-packed dish that transforms humble zucchini into a creamy, golden delight. Thinly sliced zucchini is layered with a rich mixture of eggs, cream, and cheeses like cheddar or mozzarella, often combined with breadcrumbs or crushed crackers for a satisfying crunch. Baked until bubbly and lightly browned, this casserole delivers a warm, cheesy texture with the mild, fresh flavor of zucchini.

This dish is highly versatile, allowing additions like onions, garlic, or herbs such as dill or parsley to enhance its savory profile. Perfect as a side dish or a vegetarian main course, Cheesy Zucchini Casserole is a crowd-pleaser that makes great use of summer’s abundant zucchini harvest. Its hearty yet light nature makes it a comforting option for family dinners or potlucks, especially when paired with a crisp salad or crusty bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread, cubed
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 2 cups cubed zucchini
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt or to taste/li>
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place bread cubes in a medium bowl and pour melted butter over top. Add zucchini, onion, egg, and garlic salt; mix well.
  3. Transfer the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish and top with cheese.
  4. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Uncover the dish and bake for another 30 minutes.
