Prosciutto e Melone

Description

Prosciutto e Melone is a classic Italian appetizer that pairs the delicate sweetness of ripe melon, typically cantaloupe or honeydew, with the salty, savory flavor of thinly sliced prosciutto. The melon is often cut into wedges or bite-sized pieces and wrapped with strips of cured ham, creating a refreshing yet rich contrast that delights the palate. This no-cook dish is perfect for warm weather, offering a balance of juicy fruit and umami-packed prosciutto that’s both elegant and effortless.

The beauty of Prosciutto e Melone lies in its simplicity and the quality of its ingredients. A drizzle of balsamic glaze or a sprinkle of fresh mint can elevate the flavors, while the dish’s vibrant colors make it a stunning addition to any table. Served as an appetizer or light snack, it embodies the Italian philosophy of letting fresh, high-quality ingredients shine, making it a timeless favorite for gatherings or intimate dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 cantaloupe - seeded and cut into 8 wedges
  • 8 thin slices prosciutto

Steps

  1. Remove the flesh from the rind of the cantaloupe; wrap each piece of cantaloupe with a slice of prosciutto. Arrange on a platter and serve cold.
